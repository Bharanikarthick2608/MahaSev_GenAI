    Understanding the Metrics

    P-Score

    Cross-Sectoral Priority Score

    Combines health vulnerability, infrastructure strain, and resource contention into a unified priority score. Higher scores (above 7) indicate districts requiring immediate cross-sectoral intervention.

    Weighted: HVI(40%) + ISS(30%) + RCS(30%)

    HVI

    Health Vulnerability Index

    Measures health system vulnerability by analyzing ICU capacity, emergency cases, and bed occupancy. Predicts potential health crises and capacity shortfalls.

    Scale: 0-10 (Higher = More Vulnerable)

    ISS

    Infrastructure Strain Score

    Evaluates infrastructure service demand including roads, water treatment, and electricity. High scores indicate urgent infrastructure capacity expansion needs.

    Scale: 0-10 (Higher = More Strain)

    RCS

    Resource Contention Score

    Analyzes worker availability, utilization rates, and escalation patterns. Identifies districts where human resources are overstretched or underutilized.

    Scale: 0-10 (Higher = More Contention)

    SEL

    Service Equity Lag Index

    Measures service delivery disparities compared to population needs. Values above 1.3 indicate significant equity gaps requiring targeted interventions.

    Scale: 0.5-2.0+ (Higher = More Inequity)
